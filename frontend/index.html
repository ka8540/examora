What is Examora?

Examora automates the creation of course exams and assignments by analyzing professor-provided materials and student sentiment. The AI adjusts question difficulty based on feedback, ensuring balanced assessments tailored to student needs.

How It Works

  1. Step 1: Upload your course materials (PDFs, syllabi, or past exams).
  2. Step 2: Examora analyzes what topics are important and which ones have shown up in past assessments.
  3. Step 3: It checks what students say about your course (or similar courses) to understand what they find challenging.
  4. Step 4: Based on this info, it generates a set of questions tailored to the right difficulty level.
  5. Step 5: You review the questions, make changes if needed, and download your ready-to-use exam.
